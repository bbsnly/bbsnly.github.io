About

More than a decade running engineering orgs, and still in the code. The rare combination is the whole point.

I'm an engineering leader based in Copenhagen. I've been building software since 2013 and leading teams for more than a decade - as an Engineering Manager at Unity Technologies and Siteimprove, and Director of Software Engineering at VML MAP - across products and platforms serving tens of millions of users. I've also kept shipping: chartjs-php, a PHP wrapper for Chart.js I built and released, runs across 100,000+ installs.

My focus sits where technical depth meets business impact: restructuring organizations, cutting time-to-market, modernizing architecture, and bringing AI into how teams actually work. Most teams have run the AI experiments by now. Few have it reliably in the development loop six months later - embedded agentic workflows, the agent guides your team works from (CLAUDE.md, AGENTS.md), reusable skills, and the org changes that make any of it stick. That gap is where I do my best work, and I bring it to companies and the engineers growing into leaders alongside them.
